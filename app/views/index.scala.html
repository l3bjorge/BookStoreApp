About us

Who We Are

Dare to Share is an online platform which purpose is to facilitate the exchange of culture between users by offering an easy way to contact other user to sell or exchange books with no problem at all

We are a small group of programmers with big ambition and we want to make a exchange platform as big as we possibly can.

What We Do

We are constantly innovating in new projects and ideas for different ways of exchanging knowledge between people as easily as we can. Dare to Share will be improving as we come up with new methods to make it bigger so that we can finally achieve our goals

  • Find any book you need
  • Message users and negociate exchanges or deals
  • Create your own profile
  • Publish any book you want to share
